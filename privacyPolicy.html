Your Data Protection Rights

Regarding the personal information we maintain about you, you possess specific entitlements. These rights include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Right of Access: You are entitled to request a copy of the personal data we process about you, along with details concerning its usage.
  • Right to Rectification: Should you identify any inaccuracies or incompleteness in your personal data held by us, you have the right to request amendments or updates to this information.
  • Right to Erasure (Deletion): Under specific conditions, you may request the removal of your personal data. Following a verified deletion request and identity confirmation, we will expeditiously and permanently expunge your personal information from our active databases, except where legal mandates or legitimate business requirements necessitate its retention.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may seek to obtain your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, or request its direct transfer to another service provider, where technically feasible.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the option to request a temporary halt to the processing of your data under certain circumstances, for example, if you dispute its accuracy or object to its processing.
  • Right to Object: You are entitled to object to the processing of your data in particular situations, including its use for direct marketing purposes or other applications permissible by law.

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a formal data subject request via our designated communication channels. We will undertake identity verification prior to processing any request to ensure the continued security and confidentiality of your information. All legitimate requests will be processed in accordance with our internal data governance policies and within applicable statutory timeframes.
